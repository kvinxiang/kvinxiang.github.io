Truist Coach

Winning the Truist challenge track at UGAHacks X, this project combined a customized LLM with data visuals to provide users with financial guidance. I worked mainly on the backend, where I learned how to effectively prompt engineer GPT-4 to give customized responses. This was also my first taste of JavaScript, where I learned how to connect the frontend and backends of our projects using Flask. Overall, a great experience!
